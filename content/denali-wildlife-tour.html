Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from a standard park tour?

Wildlife tours use routes and timing optimised for animal sightings rather than maximum distance.

What is the best season?

June through September. July and August typically offer the highest activity.

Can children join?

Yes, though patience is needed during quiet stretches between sightings.

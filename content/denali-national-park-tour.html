Frequently Asked Questions

How far does the bus go?

Options range from shorter turnaround trips to Eielson Visitor Center at Mile 66. Deeper routes require lottery permits.

Will I see wildlife?

Wildlife is never guaranteed, but this route offers among the best odds in Alaska.

What should I bring?

Layers, rain jacket, binoculars, snacks and a camera with zoom lens.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a driving licence?

Requirements vary by operator; most accept standard licences with minimum age limits.

What should I wear?

Closed-toe shoes, long pants and layers. Operators provide helmets and often gloves.

Can two people share one ATV?

Some tours offer double machines; confirm when booking.

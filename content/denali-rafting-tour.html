Frequently Asked Questions

Is rafting safe for children?

Scenic floats welcome younger guests; whitewater has age and size minimums.

Will I get wet?

On scenic floats, usually minimally. Whitewater trips expect splashing — dress accordingly.

When does rafting season run?

Typically June through August when river levels and weather are suitable.

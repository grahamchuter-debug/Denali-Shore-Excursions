The Alaska Railroad Denali Star connects Anchorage, Talkeetna, Denali and Fairbanks on a scenic route through interior Alaska. For cruise passengers who prefer not to drive, rail is the classic way to reach the park.

Routes and Timing

Anchorage to Denali

Departs mid-morning, arrives early evening (~8 hours). Glass-dome cars available for panoramic views.

Denali to Fairbanks

Continues north from Denali, arriving Fairbanks mid-afternoon (~4 hours).

Talkeetna stop

Optional stop for flightseeing or an overnight in this historic mountaineering town.

Tips for Cruise Passengers

  • ✓Book early for peak summer dates and dome-car seating.
  • ✓Allow a full travel day — rail is slower than driving but far more scenic.
  • ✓Coordinate luggage if you are connecting from a cruise port transfer.
